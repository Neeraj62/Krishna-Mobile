Our Story

Krishna Mobile & Repair started with one goal — make quality mobile repair affordable for everyone in Pimpri. We believe that a broken screen shouldn't cost as much as a new phone, and software crashes shouldn't lead to buying replacements.

We explain everything in simple, honest language. Whether your screen is broken, phone is hanging, or you need a second-hand phone — we are here to help. You will get a upfront cost assessment, original replacement components, and quick turnaround.
